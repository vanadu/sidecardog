New York, NY
 
 
 
The Sidecar Dog Tour 2022 kicks off at the Center of the Known Universe, Times Square, New York City on Home Opening Day, 2022. From there, we ride to Yankee Stadium for peanuts and Cracker Jack!
 
 
 
 
 
 
Los Angeles, CA
 
 
 
The Sidecar Dog Tour 2022 wraps up after the final L.A. home game at Dodger Stadium. Then we head to the nearest dog-friendly beach for some Frisbee, fun and well-deserved treats!
